Fluffy Vegan Pancakes

15 minutes

This is a simple vegan pancake recipe that has been the highlight of many a Sunday brunch. A version made with chocolate chips in particular is a fan favorite of my partner. The fluffiness is mainly achieved though the inclusion of apple cider vinegar which lends an airiness to the pancakes. Makes approximately 6 medium sized pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup flour
  • 2 cup plant milk
  • 2 tbsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine flour baking powder and sugar in mixing bowl. Blend together
  2. Combine plant milk with apple cider vinegar and vanilla extract. Stir and let sit for 30 seconds
  3. Mix dry and liquid ingredients together with a fork, blending until smooth. To thicken add small amounts of flour til the consistency is correct. Add plant milk to thin.
  4. If using chocolate chips or blueberries stir into batter last
  5. Grease a frying pan with olive oil or vegan butter and pour batter onto the frying pan, cooking on medium heat. When entire pancake is occupied by bubbles flip pancake and cook until browned. Cook one at a time until all batter is used up.
  6. Top with vegan butter and maple syrup
