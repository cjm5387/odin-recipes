Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Estimated Time: 45 minutes

I've always been a fan of soup. One of my favorites was the broccoli cheddar soup from Panera Bread. So after going vegan I diligently worked on how I could possibly recreate a vegan version. This recipe was birthed out of an adaptation of vegan cheese recipe. I thought that the vegan potato and carrot cheese would lend itself perfectly to be the base of a cheddar soup. So utilizing some of the same ingredients as our vegan cheese we added some coconut milk to add some extra creaminess.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups diced potato
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup diced broccoli
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 medium onion diced
  • 2 cups vegetable stock
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder

    • Instructions

    1. Bring 4 quarts of water to a rapid boil, add chopped potatoes and carrots and boil for 20 mins
    2. 10 mins into the potatoes and carrots boiling begin sauteing onions with olive oil for 3 mins then add minced garlic and saute for 3 more mins. Add spices and lemon juice near the end of sauteing
    3. Drain the water from the potatoes and carrots. Combine with sauted mixture and add vegetable stock, coconut milk, and nutritional yeast. Cook on low heat for 10 mins
    4. Use an immersion blender or regular blender to blend the soup. Return back to the stove add chopped broccoli and cook on medium low heat for 10 mins.
    5. Serve with fresh bread or get creative and serve in a bread bowl! Stays for 4-5 in fridge or freeze to keep longer.
